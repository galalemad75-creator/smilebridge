How to Delete Your Data

To request deletion of all your data from the SmileBridge platform:

  1. Open the app and log in
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Request Account Deletion
  4. Confirm the request

Or Contact Us Directly

📧 Email: privacy@smilebridge.com

⏱️ Deletion is completed within 30 days of receiving the request

What Gets Deleted?

  • Your personal account information (name, email, phone)
  • All uploaded dental photos
  • Offer and message history
  • Reviews you have submitted
  • Any other data associated with your account
