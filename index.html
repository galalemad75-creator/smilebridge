About Us

About SmileBridge

SmileBridge is a smart digital platform that makes it easy for patients to access orthodontic services by connecting them with the best specialists in a transparent and simple way.


🎯 Our Mission

We empower every patient to access the best orthodontic care at competitive prices, through a platform that lets doctors compete to offer the best deals.


👁️ Our Vision

To become the leading platform in the Middle East for connecting orthodontic patients with specialists, ensuring quality and transparency at every step.


💡 How It Works

  • 📸 The patient takes photos of their teeth from the specified medical angles
  • 📋 The case is posted anonymously on the platform
  • 👨‍⚕️ Specialists review the case and submit their offers
  • 💰 The patient compares offers (price, duration, ratings) and chooses
  • ⭐ After completion, the patient rates their experience

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the platform:

  • Account Info: Name, email, phone number, account type (patient/doctor)
  • Dental Photos: Photos you upload to present your case to doctors
  • Doctor Info: Specialty, years of experience, qualifications, licenses
  • Activity Log: Submitted offers, messages, reviews

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing platform services and connecting patients with doctors
  • Improving user experience and developing the platform
  • Sending notifications about new offers and appointments
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing misuse

3. Health Data Protection

As a platform providing health-related services, we take special care to protect your health data:

  • Dental photos are encrypted during transfer and storage using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Photos are shown to doctors anonymously (without the patient's real name) until an offer is accepted
  • We apply security standards aligned with best practices for health data protection (HIPAA as reference)
  • We do not use your health data for marketing or sell it to third parties
  • Completed case photos are automatically deleted 12 months after case closure

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With the selected doctor after offer acceptance (contact information)
  • When legally required by authorized entities
  • Technical service providers (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. After a deletion request:

  • All personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • We may retain some anonymized (aggregated) data for statistical purposes
  • We retain financial transaction records legally required for 7 years

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience and remember your preferences. You can manage them through your browser settings.


8. Contact

For any privacy inquiries: privacy@smilebridge.com

Terms of Service

Terms of Service — Last updated: April 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using the SmileBridge platform, you agree to comply with these terms and conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.


2. Registration & Age Requirements

  • You must be 18 years or older to register
  • Users aged 13-17: requires written parental consent
  • The platform is not available to users under 13
  • Provide accurate and truthful information during registration
  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Doctors: must provide proof of qualifications and professional licenses

3. Patient Obligations

  • Take clear photos from the specified angles
  • Do not use fake photos or images from other sources
  • Inform the doctor of any health conditions that may affect treatment
  • Adhere to agreed-upon appointments and treatment plans

4. Doctor Obligations

  • Provide realistic and appropriate offers for each case
  • Maintain patient data confidentiality
  • Do not provide final diagnoses without direct clinical examination
  • Comply with professional ethics and applicable laws

5. Liability

SmileBridge is a mediation platform only and is not a healthcare provider. We are not responsible for:

  • The quality or outcome of treatment provided by doctors
  • Any disputes arising between patient and doctor
  • Diagnoses or treatments suggested through the platform

6. Payments & Fees

The platform is currently free for patients. Payment is made directly to the doctor based on the agreed offer. Premium doctor fees (in the future) will be determined by subscription plans.


7. Account Termination & Data Deletion

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates these terms, misuses the platform, or provides false information.

To request deletion of your account and all your data:

  • From within the app: Settings → Data & Account Management → Request Account Deletion

  • Deletion is completed within 30 days of the request
  • Some aggregated data may be retained for statistical purposes without linking to your identity
